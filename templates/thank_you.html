How will your data be used?


This user study is part of a larger research project aimed at exploring how news content can be adapted to better suit different user groups. In this study, we use OpenAI’s ChatGPT-4 to generate satirical summaries of real-world news articles. By having users evaluate these summaries, we seek to gain insights into consumer perceptions of the model’s ability to create engaging and funny satire. We also investigate whether receiving certain news categories in a more entertaining format still provides an effective way to distribute information, and assess the model’s ability to preserve the factual integrity of the original articles while altering the original tone.


Our end goal with this research is to discover ways to make more "hard" news— which some people tend to avoid but still contain critical societal information— more digestible and appealing.
