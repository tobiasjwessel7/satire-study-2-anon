What is this study about?

This study explores how changing the presentation of news article summaries can affect the readerâ€™s experience. During the study, you will select your preference from a total of nine different categories. After making your selection, you will be asked to read two versions of five different news article summaries and then choose your preferred version.

What do you need to do?

  • Step 1: Complete a short questionnaire about yourself (e.g., age, news habits).
  • Step 2: Choose your preferences from a total of nine news categories.
  • Step 3: Review two different versions of a news article summary and select your preferred version. Repeat this step five times.
  • Step 4: Answer questions about each of your choices and the news categories from which you reviewed summaries.

The article summaries used in this study are collected from a selection of the most-read U.S.-based outlets and categories.

Additional Information about the Study

Your participation is voluntary, and all responses are confidential and processed anonymously. The entire process takes about 13 minutes. Thank you for your contribution!
